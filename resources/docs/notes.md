# Notes

Notes allow staff to record interactions, observations, and follow-up reminders tied to individual contacts. A good note log creates a shared relationship history that the whole team can reference.

## Notes List

The Notes index shows all notes across all contacts. You can filter by contact or date to find specific interactions.

## Adding a Note

Notes can be created from the Notes index or directly from a contact record's detail view.

- **Contact** (required) — the contact this note is about.
- **Body** — the content of the note. Be specific: include dates, what was discussed, and any agreed-upon next steps.
- **Date** — when the interaction occurred (defaults to today).

## Best Practices

- Record notes promptly after calls or meetings while details are fresh.
- Use consistent language across the team so notes are searchable and understandable by anyone.
- Avoid storing sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary for your mission.

## Viewing a Contact's Notes

Open any contact record to see all notes associated with that contact in chronological order.
