# Dashboard

The Dashboard is the first screen you see when you log into the admin panel. It provides a high-level view of what is happening across your organization.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Account summary** — your logged-in user name and role.
- **Recent activity** — a quick look at recently added contacts, upcoming events, and new donations.

## Getting Around

Use the left-hand navigation to move between sections of the admin panel. Navigation is grouped into **CRM**, **CMS**, **Finance**, **Tools**, and **Settings**. Groups can be collapsed and expanded.

If you are unsure where to find something, look for the section heading that best matches your task — contacts and households live under CRM, pages and events under CMS, and donations under Finance.
