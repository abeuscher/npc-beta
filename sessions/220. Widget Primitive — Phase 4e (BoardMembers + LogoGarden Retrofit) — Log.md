### Notable findings (carried into 221's design decisions)

- **Architectural milestone landed.** With 220 closing, the `WidgetRenderer::render()` line 46 legacy loop has zero callers in `app/Widgets/*`, and `WidgetDataResolver::resolveCustom` is unreferenced from the renderer. The slot-strip mechanism in `PageBuilderApiController::filterQueryConfig` becomes formally retirable. **All three are scheduled for deletion in 221** (the Phase 4 follower) along with the `WidgetDataResolver::resolveCustom` private helpers and the `widget_type_collections` field on the Vue API surface.
- **Tag relations are uniform.** Cross-referencing `Page`, `Event`, `CollectionItem`, `Organization`, `Contact` confirmed all five declare `tags(): MorphToMany` with the identical `morphToMany(Tag::class, 'taggable')` shape. The `cmsTags` relation references at [WidgetDataResolver.php:70](app/Services/WidgetDataResolver.php#L70) and [:74](app/Services/WidgetDataResolver.php#L74) are stale (relation deleted in session 040 during the cms_tags → tags consolidation) — `include_tags`/`exclude_tags` on the legacy path has been silently broken for ~14 months without anyone noticing. Goes away when `resolveCustom` is deleted in 221; not a separate fix.
- **Carousel-Settings is a misnamed advanced-fields chevron.** During 221 scoping, traced [InspectorFieldGroup.vue:99](resources/js/page-builder-vue/components/InspectorFieldGroup.vue#L99): the heading `"Carousel Settings"` is hardcoded on a generic "advanced fields" accordion fed by the `advanced: true` flag on individual config-schema fields. This means *every* widget whose schema marks any field as advanced has been showing a chevron-collapsed section literally titled "Carousel Settings" — including BoardMembers, BarChart, etc. The label has been wrong on most widgets for months. 221 removes the chevron and renames the heading.
- **Five knobs uniformly across all list-shaped widgets.** During 221 scoping, the design point of "should BlogListing/EventsListing get just `limit` or the full five-knob set?" resolved to the full set. Per-source plumbing cost is bounded; UX uniformity wins. Captured as a new feedback memory: `feedback_uniform_ux_over_per_type_plumbing.md`.

## Deferred decisions

- **Phase 4 follower — Query settings in the contract layer.** Drafted as session 221 (`sessions/221. base-prompt.md` + session prompt). Scope landed during 220's close: extend `ContractResolver` per source-arm with `order_by`/`direction`/`include_tags`/`exclude_tags`; add `App\WidgetPrimitive\QuerySettings` value object; expose `query_settings: { has_panel, order_by_options, supports_tags }` on the per-widget API; flatten `query_config` keying from slot-keyed to top-level (one-time data migration); delete `WidgetDataResolver::resolveCustom`, the `WidgetRenderer::render()` line 46 legacy loop, `PageBuilderApiController::filterQueryConfig`, the `widget_type_collections` field, and the chevron toggles in `QuerySettings.vue` and `InspectorFieldGroup.vue`. Five knobs uniform across all list-shaped widgets (no per-source variance). The tag implementation is uniform via the `tags()` relation — no per-arm variance there either.
- **Real "carousel-shape" abstraction.** During 221 scoping, the user observed that QuerySettings and a hypothetical CarouselSettings could be parallel system-derived panels. There is no abstract "carousel-shape widget" concept in the codebase today. Designing this is bigger than 221 and shouldn't ride alongside the query-settings architecture work. Deferred indefinitely — reopen if Phase 4 retrofit progresses far enough that a real shape pattern emerges.
- **`date_range` as a user-configurable knob.** EventsListing and ThisWeeksEvents lock `date_range` to identity-defining defaults. Exposing it requires UX design (date pickers, named presets). Not in the universal five-knob set. Future session.
- **`WidgetDataResolver::resolveProducts` retirement.** Still called by `ProductCarousel/template.blade.php` directly. Retires when ProductCarousel's retrofit lands — somewhere in the 5 remaining widget retrofits (BlogPager, EventDescription, EventRegistration, ProductDisplay, ProductCarousel).
- **Widget-owns-demo-data architecture.** Surfaced in 218; the threshold of "5–6+ widgets contract-locked" is now firmly crossed (10 contract-locked after 220). Right time to formalize is when the user signals it as a priority. Not in scope for 221.
- **Default `vendor/bin/pest` memory-limit override.** Fifth consecutive session using `-d memory_limit=2G` to sidestep the latent mid-suite OOM (Spatie GD image processing). Whether to bake into project tooling (PHP ini, composer script, `tests/Pest.php` setup) remains a separate question.
- **5 remaining page-builder widgets after 220.** BlogPager (cheapest; post projector exists), the Event trio (EventDescription, EventRegistration; EventCalendar is client-side rendered and out of Phase 4 scope), and the Product pair (ProductCarousel + ProductDisplay; require a new `'product'` arm in `ContractResolver` and `SystemModelProjector`). 222 candidate after 221's follower work lands.
