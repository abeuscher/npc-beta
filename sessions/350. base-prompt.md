## Process rules for every session

Base-prompt process rules apply in full. Session-specific:

- **Rich-text transforms are the high-blast-radius change** — the OL/UL→`<ul>` and heading-`<strong>`-strip fixes touch *every* rich-text surface (widgets, notes, events, email templates, collections, custom fields). Settle save-time vs render-time placement at the Open Gate, and cover the change with Pest at the transform boundary before touching anything else. A render-time transform is non-destructive (doesn't mutate stored HTML); a save-time transform fixes stored content going forward but is a permanent rewrite — name the tradeoff explicitly.
- **The `contactEmail` leak is a privacy fix, not just SEO** — pull it from the public contact-email setting (the operator-set address), or remove it from client output entirely if nothing on the page consumes `window.__site.contactEmail`. Grep for consumers before deleting.
- **Image eager/`fetchpriority` is scoped to the LCP candidates** (the `Image` + `EventImage` widgets) for v1 — a per-instance eager/lazy toggle and/or auto-eager the first image on a page, plus `fetchpriority="high"` on the chosen eager image. Do **not** sweep all 15 `loading="lazy"` sites; that's broader than this session.
- **Builds:** `npm run build` after any `resources/js`/`resources/scss` change; `docker compose exec app php artisan build:public` after any widget `styles.scss` or asset change. Most of this session is Blade/PHP (no build), but the Nav/image template changes may not need a build — match the touched path to the rule in `docs/app-reference.md`.
- **Settle the open questions at session start** (rich-text transform placement; hero-eager strategy; whether `og:title` needs a fix or only a verification) — log them.
