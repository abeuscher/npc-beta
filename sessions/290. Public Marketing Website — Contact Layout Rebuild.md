## Open questions to resolve at session start

These are small surfaces. Defaults below are reasonable; only pause if a decision would propagate.

- **Optional "What I respond to fastest" band — include or omit?** Surface for user. Default recommendation: **include it** — it's a quiet way to communicate the founder's working pattern without lengthening the page much, and it pairs with the anti-SaaS voice. But the call is the user's.
- **Hero image — yes or no?** Copy.md doesn't specify. The other three pages (Home / About / Pricing) all carry a hero portrait. Including one here keeps the structural rhyme but may make Contact feel disproportionately heavy for a short page. Default recommendation: **include the hero portrait** for visual consistency across pages; pick a fourth distinct portrait from the sample-image library (not duplicating the home / about / pricing picks).
- **Email-band layout.** A 2-column hero-like band (text + image) reads as marketing; a centered 1-column band reads as a directory entry. Default: 1-column centered, since the band's job is to make the email address scannable. Surface if a Contact spec lands with a different opinion.
- **Background tones across bands.** Default: hero gradient (same as Home / About / Pricing for structural rhyme); Email band white; "What I respond to fastest" band tinted `#d4d4f2` if included. Don't introduce new background colors.
