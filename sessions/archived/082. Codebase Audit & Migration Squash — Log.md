- Session 081 agreed to block Fund and Product deletion when referenced records exist, but this was not implemented. Wrote and ran migration `2026_03_28_082001_add_missing_fk_indexes.php`. `contacts.household_id` was initially included but removed after discovering it already existed in the session-062 schema dump.
