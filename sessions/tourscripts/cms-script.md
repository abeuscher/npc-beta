CMS
The CMS, or content management system, is a tool for managing the pages on your website. 


Widgets
The CMS features a WYSIWYG style editor and block builder made out of components called “widgets”. Widget can be customized and also added to. The can display content from a table of items or through individual fields edited on page. 


Forms
The CMS contains forms and form handling. Custom forms can be added, and the forms needed to manage donations, membership, and newsletter subscriptions are supplied as blocks that can be added to any or all pages.


Templates
Pages are based on templates. Templates can be saved from an existing page, imported, or created. Each template contains a customized header, footer, and page body. 


Theming
The site theme is controlled from a central location. Text styles and spacing are controlled as well as basic colors and button styling.


Custom Snippets
If you are installing any advanced tracking scripts, you will be happy to know that every page has individually editable header and footer snippets, and that snippets can also be added at the site level. This gives you the fine tuned ability you need to install complex tracking and analytics.


Conclusion
This CMS was built to do what you need it to, and to be extensible in every direction. It is approachable and not daunting to get used to, but if you do want advanced functionality, it is there to reach for. As the product matures, the widgets available to the product will grow, but they are already able to provide a fully customized integrated website with a consistent look and feel.
