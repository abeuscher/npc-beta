### Phase 2 — Classify the residuals

- **`page-builder/layout-inspector.spec.ts:26` — UNEXPECTED (the only real failure).** Times out waiting for `.layout-inspector .inspector-tabs` `getByRole('button', { name: 'Column Settings' })`. An explicit `role="tab"` overrides the implicit `button` role, so `getByRole('button', …)` can no longer match the three tabs. The spec was last touched at A005 (`4e2d575`), before the sweep — a test that wasn't re-run after an accessibility improvement landed. **Verdict: decision-tree (a)** — the product change is deliberate and correct; the spec updates to match.
- **`page-builder/inline-editing-foundation.spec.ts:79` — flaky (retry-recovered).** The known page-builder cold-load `page.waitForURL` flake A003 deferred and A005 left on passive watch. Within the project's standard `retries: 1` tolerance; no action. (Did not reproduce at all on the verification run.)
